Set up your computer

In order to collect data on your tablet via excel, you need two things:

  1. To stay organised and
  2. Excel

Once you have those, you are ready to prepare your field sheets.

Preparing field sheets

Preparing field sheets to collect data on a tablet is very similar to preparing regular field sheets. In fact, the first steps are the same: download a field sheet from ForestPlots and format it so that you can see only the columns that you want to be able to work with.

The next step is slightly different. Rather than set your print area, now you are going to lock columns that you do not want to accidentally modify in the field.
