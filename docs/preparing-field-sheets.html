Module 6 Preparing field sheets

Learning objectives

  • Review how to create a field sheet on ForestPlots.
  • Learn how to lock columns.
  • Practice collecting data on an Excel field sheet.

 

Preparing field sheets on ForestPlots

If you already know how to prepare field sheets through the ForestPlots database, skip ahead to the next section.

If you need a refresher on how to create field sheets, watch this video or visit page 174 of the ForestPlots Manual.

If you’ve never been shown how to make field sheets before by a ForestPlots team member, reach out to the data officer of the country that you will be collecting data in and request training (see Module 2).

Preparing excel sheets for the tablet

Preparing excel sheets to collect data on a tablet is very similar to preparing regular field sheets. In fact, the first step is the same: - download a field sheet from ForestPlots,
- and format the field sheet to only see the columns that you want to work with.

The next step is slightly different. Rather than set your print area, now you are going to lock columns that you do not want to accidentally modify in the field.
