QGIS Training

Module 13 Plot XYZ: QGIS practice

Learning objectives

  • Practice what you’ve learned

 

The only way to master a skill is to practice. So let’s practice.

insert name has requested that all field teams collect data for a new research project. It is your job to successfully collect this data and send it to the insert cool group for them to upload the data to the database and analyse it.

1. On a piece of paper, write down the steps you will take to get the data all the way through sending your collected data to your colleague. Show to one of the instructors before moving on

2. The plot that you will be working with has previously been re-censused. You can see a plot dump of the data here (insert attached excel document). Based on the information, why will we collect this data via QGIS? Hint: why did we have to collect data in Module 8 in Excel?

3. Create a folder called XYZ_plot_practice in your QGIS_training folder.

4. Here is the geopackage that you will need to generate the QGIS survey. Save this file in your XYZ_plot_practice folder.

5. Create a QGIS package called XYZ_collection.

6. Transfer the XYZ_data_collection package to your tablet and collect the data.

7. Transfer your collected data back onto your laptop into your XYZ_plot_practice folder so that you have your original XYZ_data_collection package, your collected data in a file called XYZ_data_collected, and your newly collected data called XYZ_complete.xlsx.

8. Review the XYZ_complete excel file. Do you notice any errors? What post-processing will you have to do?

9. Now that you have the data on your computer, what would you do next?

10. Show your XYZ_complete file to your instructor and show them your answer to number 9.
